Soup

How to Soup

Food Ingredients

  • four cartons of soup stock
  • 10g of salt
  • anything else that is edible

Steps

  1. Boil stock.
  2. Toss everything edible into stock.
  3. Wait hours, until all ingredients are heated to your liking.
  4. Apply salt.
  5. Check if soup.
  6. If ingredients are not submerged in liquid, it is not soup. Toss and try again.
